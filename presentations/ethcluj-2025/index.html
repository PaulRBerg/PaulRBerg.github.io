📈 Classic TVL: The Ultimate Vanity Metric

  • 💸 Price × Token Count = Magic Number
  • 🎭 Makes protocols look bigger than they are
  • 🤡 $100M TVL for $10M market cap? Seems legit!
  • 📊 DAO treasuries and vested tokens = inflated
